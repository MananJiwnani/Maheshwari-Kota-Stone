About Us

Welcome to Great India Kota Stone, your trusted partner in the finest Kota stone, limestone, and Mandana sandstone. With over 45 years of industry expertise and more than 7,500 happy customers, we take pride in providing the best quality and service. Our extensive range of products covers all types of Kota stone, ensuring that we meet every need you have. Whether you're looking for specific sizes or thicknesses, we've got you covered. All sizes and thicknesses are available to suit any project, big or small. Operating with two state-of-the-art factories—Maheshwari Stone Corporation and Ramganjmandi Stone Polishing Industries—we guarantee precision, durability, and excellence in every stone we deliver. Let us help you find the perfect stone solution for your next project!
